Childhood & Musical influences

CLOSE ME

The music dream started early.

My mom, who was a musician, thought I would follow my brother: just sit back in my throne and march to the beat of the drums. No way. At five, I chose a pretty dress and lovely hair.

That's how I started playing the charming violin.

The Strings Sisters


Trio col Basso


This is my trio. Me and the sisters: Freja and Ida.

Ever since we started playing we experienced very special energy.


Together we feel the impulse to continuously improve our musical skills.


It brings us so much joy and inspiration.


Also, it is just the right feeling to keep when it gets tough in the education.


Our group is deeply moved by this mantra:

"Touch and surprise the audience"

CHANGE OF PLANS

Before high school, I decided that I wanted to do more stuff and fill my time with other activities. During this period, I was spending less and less time with the violin.

The joy in music was slowly fading away… The dream of becoming a violinist got lost in the turmoil of new and different experiences…

A Hard Goodbye

My talented composer friend, Tine, went away after three years.


Looking back, I can see her as one of the most special influencers in my life.

I have a lot of happy memories of us playing together and enjoying those moments without any pressure. It was hard to let her go after such a beautiful period in our lives.
